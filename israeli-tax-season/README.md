# Israeli Tax Season Bundle

Tax season in Israel means navigating annual returns, bimonthly VAT reports, withholding reconciliation, and finding every legitimate deduction. This bundle combines five skills that cover the full tax workflow for individuals, freelancers, and businesses.

## Who is this for?

- **Freelancers** filing annual tax returns (tofes 1301)
- **Business owners** managing VAT reports and corporate tax
- **Employees with side income** who need to file independently
- **Accountants** looking for AI-assisted tax preparation tools

## What you get

1. **Israeli Tax Returns** -- prepare and understand annual tax filings
2. **Israeli VAT Reporting** -- automate bimonthly VAT calculations and reports
3. **Israeli Tax Withholding** -- manage nikui mas b'makor and reconcile withheld amounts
4. **Israeli Expense Categorizer** -- sort expenses into correct tax deduction categories
5. **Israeli Corporate Tax Strategy** -- optimize tax planning for businesses

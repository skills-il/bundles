# Government Navigator Bundle

Israeli bureaucracy is famously complex. Between government offices, Bituach Leumi, municipal services, and an endless stream of forms, even simple tasks can take hours of research and waiting. This bundle brings together five AI skills that help you navigate the system, understand your rights, and get things done faster.

## Who is this for?

- **New immigrants (olim)** learning to navigate Israeli government systems
- **Citizens** dealing with Bituach Leumi, municipal offices, or government forms
- **Small business owners** handling permits, licenses, and regulatory requirements
- **Anyone** who has ever been sent to the wrong government office

## What you get

1. **Israeli Bureaucracy Decoder** -- describe your situation and get a clear roadmap: which office, what documents, what to expect
2. **Israeli Bituach Leumi** -- understand your national insurance benefits, eligibility, and how to apply
3. **Israeli Gov Form Automator** -- help filling out government forms with field-by-field guidance and validation
4. **Israel Gov API** -- access open government data and services programmatically
5. **Israeli Fines Fighter** -- navigate the appeals process for traffic and parking fines

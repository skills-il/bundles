# Small Business Starter Bundle

Running a small business in Israel involves a constant pile of administrative work: issuing legally compliant invoices, tracking every shekel in and out, calculating employee salaries, chasing late payments, and keeping customers happy. This bundle gives you five AI skills that handle the most painful parts of business operations, so you can focus on what you actually do.

Whether you opened a shop last month or have been running your business for years, these skills speak the language of Israeli business -- electronic invoices through the Tax Authority clearance system, bituach leumi rates, and Hebrew customer communication built in from the start.

## Who is this for?

- **New business owners** (osek murshe) looking to set up efficient systems from day one
- **Shop owners and service providers** who handle their own books or want to reduce accountant hours
- **Small employers** with a handful of employees who need accurate payroll without expensive software
- **Anyone** who has ever felt uncomfortable chasing a client for an overdue payment

## What you get

Five skills covering the full operations loop of a small Israeli business:

1. **Israeli E-Invoice** -- issue tax-compliant electronic invoices cleared through the Israeli Tax Authority system
2. **Israeli Bookkeeping Automation** -- automated day-to-day transaction recording and expense categorization
3. **Israeli Payroll Calculator** -- calculate net salary, bituach leumi, keren hishtalmut, and pension contributions
4. **Israeli Client Payment Chaser** -- polite, professional payment reminders that escalate automatically
5. **Israeli Customer Support Automator** -- handle common customer inquiries in Hebrew without manual effort

# Legal & Compliance Bundle

Israeli law touches every part of running a business -- from the first employment contract you sign, to the privacy policy on your website, to the cancellation terms in your online store. Staying compliant is not optional, but finding reliable answers to specific legal questions is time-consuming and expensive. This bundle provides five AI skills that give you accurate, up-to-date guidance on the Israeli legal questions that come up most often.

These skills are designed to help lawyers work faster, help compliance officers stay ahead of regulatory changes, and help business owners understand their obligations without needing to call a lawyer for every small question.

## Who is this for?

- **Lawyers and legal advisors** looking to speed up document drafting and research on Israeli law
- **HR and compliance officers** managing employment law and data privacy obligations
- **E-commerce business owners** who need to ensure their online store meets Israeli consumer protection law
- **Landlords and tenants** dealing with rental agreements under Israeli law

## What you get

Five skills covering the most common areas of Israeli legal and compliance work:

1. **Israeli Employment Contracts** -- generate and review employment agreements that meet Israeli statutory requirements
2. **Israeli Privacy Shield** -- navigate Israeli privacy law and prepare for the upcoming GDPR-aligned reform
3. **Israeli E-Commerce Compliance** -- audit your online store against Israeli consumer protection and e-commerce regulations
4. **Israeli Rental Agreements** -- draft and understand residential and commercial rental contracts under Israeli law
5. **Israeli Workplace Rights Navigator** -- look up employee rights, employer obligations, and common labor law questions
